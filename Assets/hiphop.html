HipHop

Hip hop or hip-hop, also known as rap, and formerly known as disco rap, is a genre of popular music
that originated in the early 1970s by black African Americans and Caribbean Americans immigrants
in the Bronx, a borough of New York City. It existed for several years prior to mainstream discovery.
